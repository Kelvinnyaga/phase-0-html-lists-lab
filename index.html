ngredients required for making a grilled cheese sandwich

    2 slices of bread 4 slices of cheese>
      Cheddar, Mozzarella, and pepper jack
     1 tsp of butter

Ordered list of for creating grilled cheese

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. turn over and fry for 2 minutes
